TOP 10 BOOKS OF INDIA

1. Wings of Fire

Wings of Fire

Author: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

An inspiring autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, Indias former President and Missile Man.

2. The God of Small Things

The God of Small Things

Author: Arundhati Roy

A beautifully crafted story about family, love, and societal norms set in Kerala, India.

3. The White Tiger

The White Tiger

Author: Aravind Adiga

An exploration of India's social and economic divide through the eyes of an ambitious driver in Delhi.

4. Midnight's Children

Midnight's Children

Author: Salman Rushdie

A magical realist novel intertwining the story of Indias independence with the life of one boy born at midnight.

5. Train to Pakistan

Train to Pakistan

Author: Khushwant Singh

A gripping tale set during the Partition, capturing human suffering and resilience.

6. The Guide

The Guide

Author: R.K. Narayan

A transformative journey of a tourist guide who goes from conman to holy man in an unexpected twist.

7. The Inheritance of Loss

The Inheritance of Loss

Author: Kiran Desai

An emotional exploration of cultural identity and the immigrant experience in post-colonial India.

8. A Fine Balance

A Fine Balance

Author: Rohinton Mistry

A powerful story of four characters in 1975 India, examining survival, hope, and resilience.

9. Shantaram

Shantaram

Author: Gregory David Roberts

An epic novel based on the author's life as a fugitive in Bombay and his adventures across India.

10. India After Gandhi

India After Gandhi

Author: Ramachandra Guha

A comprehensive history of independent India, offering insights into its political and social evolution.
